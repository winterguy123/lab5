A basic guide on the Hemlock Flower

What is the Hemlock?

The Hemlock flower, also known as the Conium maculatum, is a poisonous plant that can kill living things if consumed.

Where is the Hemlock?

Hemlock flowers can typically be found in sunny areas, fields, and ditches. Hemlock plants have red/purple spots and streaks, and often grow up to 10 feet tall with small white 5 petal flowers.

What does the Hemlock do?

Hemlock plants are very toxic. Even a small amount of hemlock being consumed can spell death to humans. If the plant is consumed, contact authorities quickly.

What do you do when you find a Hemlock?

  • Don't interact with the plant.
  • Contact the authorities.
  • Identify the key characteristics of the Hemlock.
